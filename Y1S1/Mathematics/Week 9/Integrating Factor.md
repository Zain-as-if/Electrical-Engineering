First Order differential equation form of $$\frac{dy}{dx}=F(x,y)$$
or: 
$$y'(x)=F(x,y(x))$$

Equations can either be linear or non-linear

Linear: 
F is linear in y, i.e.
$$F(x,y)=A(x)y$$
For some function A(x)

Non-Linear:
F is non-linear in y (infinitely many possibilities)
$$F(x,y)=A(x)y^2+B(x)y$$
$$F(x,y)=A(x)\sin y+B(x)y$$
$$F(x,y)=e^y+\frac{x}{y}$$
ETC
