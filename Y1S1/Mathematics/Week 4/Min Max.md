When $\frac{dy}{dx}=0$ called stationary point

Whether stationary point is maximum, minimum or point of inflection dependent on second derivative
$$\begin{align*}
\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}&>0\ \text{Minimum}\\
\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}&=0\ \text{Inflection Point}\\
\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}&<0\ \text{Maximum}
\end{align*}$$
