Electric field strength is a point in space the force is felt by a unit +ve electric charge

The result of a number of forces on a charge results in a vector field where each point in space has a vector force associated with it

Field lines emanate from +ve charges, terminate at -ve charges

Sometimes terminating charge must be assumed at $\infty$

If charge was truly isolated, would be no 'test' charge to establish the existence of the electric field
