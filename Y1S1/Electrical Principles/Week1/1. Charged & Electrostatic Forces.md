Charges of protons & electrons:
Electron: -1.602x$10^{-19}$ C
Proton: +1.602x$10^{-19}$ C 

Charges only come in two varieties:
+ve: protons > electrons
-ve: protons < electrons

Like charges repel
Unlike charges attract

<h3>Point Charges</h3>
- Charges which are concentrated at a mathematical point
- Hypothetical construct since the charge must be carried by a particle of matter (e.g. electron/proton) which have non-zero size
- Practically, charges can be assumed as point charges provided their extension (diameter of circumscribing sphere) is small compared to the distance between them, i.e.:
